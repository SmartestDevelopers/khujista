What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimisation. It increases website traffic to rank higher on search engines like Google by driving more organic traffic.  

Why is SEO important to your business?

It helps increase organic traffic and brand awareness by ranking your website to page 1 and leading to more leads, sales, and a stronger online presence.

Is SEO a one-time thing?

SEO is not a one-time thing. It’s an ongoing process that requires consistent effort and adaptation. Think of it like tending to a garden: regular care is essential for long-term growth and success.
